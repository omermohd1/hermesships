About Us

HERMES SHIPS SUPPLIERS

We are Complete Ship Store Suppliers (Ship Chandlers), dealing complete supplies, HERMES SHIPS SUPPLIERS is a leading provider of provisions and spare parts to ships across the Indian Sea Ports , Suez Canal , UAE , Turkey , Singapore , etc. Established in 1990, we aim to deliver the best, cost-efficient products in the shortest time possible. By directly partnering with top manufacturers in India, we offer our customers unbeatable prices and quality..

Our scope covers the supply of A to Z, Complete Ship Supplies which includes Provisions, Technical, Engine, Deck, Cabin, Safety/Anti Piracy, Wire & Mooring Ropes, Marine Chemicals & Gases, LSA FFA, Bonded Stores, etc. We also undertake various ship services like Waste Disposal, Motor Rewinding, Maintenance & Repair, Delivery of Spares, etc.
